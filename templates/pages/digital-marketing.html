Digital Marketing

Course Overview

Learn result-driven digital marketing skills to grow brands and businesses online. This practical course teaches you the strategies and tools used by leading digital marketers worldwide. Perfect for entrepreneurs, business professionals, and aspiring marketing specialists.

What You'll Learn

  • Digital marketing fundamentals and strategy
  • Social media marketing and content strategy
  • Search engine optimization (SEO)
  • Search engine marketing (SEM) and PPC advertising
  • Email marketing and automation
  • Analytics and data-driven decision making
  • Content creation and copywriting
  • Campaign planning and ROI measurement

Course Highlights

This course is 100% practical with real-world projects and case studies. You'll work with actual marketing tools like Google Analytics, Facebook Ads Manager, and more. Learn strategies that work and apply them to real brands, building a portfolio of successful campaigns.

Duration & Certification

Course Duration: 10 weeks | Full-time: 40 hours/week
Upon completion, you'll receive a nationally recognized Digital Marketing certificate and have proven campaign examples for your portfolio.
