Solar Design and Installation

Course Overview

Master solar system design and installation with hands-on, industry-ready training. This comprehensive course equips you with the knowledge and practical skills needed to design, install, and optimize solar power systems. Whether you're looking to start a career in the renewable energy sector or enhance your existing skills, this course provides the foundation you need.

What You'll Learn

  • Fundamentals of solar energy and photovoltaic technology
  • System design and sizing calculations
  • Installation techniques and best practices
  • Electrical connections and safety protocols
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Grid-tied and off-grid system configurations
  • Regulatory compliance and standards
  • Scaling your solar business operations

Course Highlights

This course combines theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on experience in our state-of-the-art training facility. You'll work with real solar components and tools under the guidance of experienced instructors. By the end of the course, you'll be confident in designing and installing complete solar systems for residential and commercial applications.

Duration & Certification

Course Duration: 6, 12, or 24 months
Upon completion, you'll receive a nationally recognized certificate approved by the Ministry of Education, giving you credibility in the renewable energy industry.
