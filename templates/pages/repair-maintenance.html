Repair & Maintenance

Course Overview

Gain practical skills to diagnose, repair, and maintain solar power systems. This hands-on course teaches you everything needed to keep solar installations running at peak efficiency. Perfect for technicians and service professionals, you'll learn troubleshooting techniques and maintenance best practices.

What You'll Learn

  • System diagnostics and troubleshooting procedures
  • Common faults and their solutions
  • Preventive maintenance schedules
  • Electrical testing and safety protocols
  • Component replacement and repairs
  • Performance monitoring and optimization
  • Customer communication and service documentation
  • Emergency response procedures

Course Highlights

This course emphasizes practical, hands-on learning with real solar equipment. You'll work on actual systems in our service center and learn from experienced technicians. The course includes extensive field work and real-world case studies to prepare you for professional service work.

Duration & Certification

Course Duration: 6, 12, or 24 months
Upon completion, you'll receive a nationally recognized certificate that qualifies you as a solar service technician.
