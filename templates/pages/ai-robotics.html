AI & Robotics

Course Overview

Build real AI and robotics solutions through practical, beginner-friendly projects. This course takes you from fundamental concepts to building working robotics systems and AI applications. Perfect for those interested in emerging technologies and automation in renewable energy.

What You'll Learn

  • Basics of artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Robotics hardware and components
  • Programming robots with Python and other languages
  • Computer vision and sensor integration
  • Autonomous system development
  • AI applications in renewable energy
  • Real-world project implementation
  • Future technologies and innovations

Course Highlights

This is a highly practical course where you'll work with actual robots and programming tools. You'll build projects from scratch, starting with simple tasks and progressing to complex systems. The course emphasizes learning by doing, with every concept reinforced through hands-on coding and robotics work.

Duration & Certification

Course Duration: 14 weeks | Full-time: 40 hours/week
Upon completion, you'll receive a nationally recognized certificate in AI and Robotics, positioning you for careers in emerging technology fields.
