About Us

Your Best Choice For Renewable Energy and Tecnology Education

NOVELSOLAR ACADEMY was established in September 2015, We are a leading institution committed to providing high-quality training in solar energy and modern renewable technologies. With over 1,000 graduates, our academy has played a major role in shaping skilled professionals who now contribute meaningfully across the renewable energy sector. We combine strong theoretical knowledge with hands-on training to ensure our students become competent, innovative, and industry-ready. Learning takes place in a fully equipped environment that includes a modern library, practical laboratory, service centre, distilling plant, product showroom, and field training facilities.

Our Mission

To inspire and prepare the next generation of solar energy leaders through accessible, high-quality education and training that strengthens skills, sparks innovation, and supports a sustainable future.

Our Vision

To promote world-class innovation driven by modern technology and entrepreneurial practices. We aim to produce graduates who can think creatively, harness technological advancements, and become leaders in both public and private sectors within Nigeria and beyond.
